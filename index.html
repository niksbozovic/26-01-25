/ABOUT/THE/PROJECT/

//THE STUDENT PROTEST MOVEMENT STARTED IN NOVEMBER 2024, FOLLOWING THE COLLAPSE OF THE CANOPY IN NOVI SAD ON NOV 1ST. THIS HAS RESULTED IN BOTH THE STUDENT BLOCKADES, AS WELL AS THE START OF A NEW TYPE OF DIRECT DEMOCRACY, KNOWN AS THE PLENUM.//

//ONE OF THE FIRST TRULY MASSIVE PROTESTS HAPPENED ON JANUARY 27TH, A 24 HOUR BLOCKADE OF AUTOKOMANDA, A VITAL INTERSECTION. IT WAS A FORM OF RADICALISATION.//

//THESE PHOTOS DOCUMENT THE DAY BEFORE THE PROTESTS, A MOMENT IN TIME WHERE EACH STICKER TELLS A STORY OF REBELLION. BY EXAMINING THE VISUAL CULTURE OF PROTESTS, WE CAN SEE WHICH ORGANISATIONS SUPPORTED US, THE LANGUAGE WE USE WHEN DESCRIBING TRAGEDIES AND THE PATTERNS OF SYMBOLS TAKING OVER BELGRADE. WE CAN SEE THE SADNESS, THE POWER, THE WILL TO SURVIVE, TO FIGHT, TO TELL STORIES, TO FEEL HUMAN CONNECTION, TO CREATE REVOLUTIONARY ART, TO LEAVE A MARK ON OUR CITY AND OUR COUNTRY. AND IN THE END TO BRING ABOUT CHANGE.//

//WE NOW LIVE IN A NEW MOMENT, A CALL FOR ELECTIONS, A CALL FOR INSTITUTIONS TO WORK AS INTENDED, WHERE PEOPLE UNITE AND FIGHT FOR OUR LIVES. THIS IS NOW. THESE STICKERS TELL OUR STORIES.//
